About Me

Hi! My name is Belle Kornberg and I am so glad that you have found my page.

Having been born and raised in Columbus, Ohio, a fashion powerhouse, I have been immersed in this industry from a young age. I have a strong passion for fashion and want to share this with others.

I am a Junior at the S.I. Newhouse School of Public Communications with a major in Graphic Design, and a minor in the iSchool.

As far as experience goes, I have been a design intern at a big retailer and have learned a lot from this. I helped design the clothing for upcoming seasons, participated in design department meetings, and learned from well-trained professionals.

I created this platform to enlighten others with my strong fascination. I hope to help those who may feel lost in the world of fashion, or want inspiration. I also hope to spread awareness of influencers and brands that people may not be aware of.

Ideally, I would like to combine my graphic design skills with my love of fashion and my newly developed skills in front-end web development to create an application that contains all of the content on this page, but is also interactive as a social network. People could meet people from around the world through their shared interests and love of the fashion world.

If you have any questions or would like to contact me please feel free to fill out the information below!
