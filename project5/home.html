Cargo Pants

Why all the craze?

If you're like me, you like to keep it simple on top. I think I own 3 of the same staple shirts in my closet (obviously only the color black), and accessorize it with other pieces of clothing: lately, it's been cargo pants. Let's face it, the y2k low-rise trends are back in, so it is our time to either own it and rock it, or ignore it and carry-on with our daily lives. I don't know what specifically brought cargos back in style, but trust me, they're back. What better way to add a touch to your perfect outfit than some patterned cargos, plain color, or cargo sweats? They have pockets, they're not skin tight, and they make you stand out! Bomber Jackets

Why all the craze?

Maybe it's because “Top Gun: Maverick” has taken over, or it's because these jackets are genuinely cool, but bomber jackets and aviator-type fashion will be everywhere this fall. Name a better jacket for a crisp fall day to go on top of your hoodie? These jackets have been in many runway shows and all over some of the biggest retailers' sites. Get your hands on one fast before people catch up to you!
